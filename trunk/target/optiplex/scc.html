Senior Companion Care

At Senior Companion Care, we strive to connect caregivers with seniors in need of care.

Filter System

With our filter system, care seekers can quickly search for their ideal caregivers based on their location, type of care and schedule.

Simple Interface

Our mission is to create a simple and easy to use interface for both caregivers and care seekers. Users can quickly navigate around the site with our excellent user interface.

Caregivers

Caregivers have their own account features which allow them to customize their profile and display their unique skills using our website.
